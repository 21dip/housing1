NAVRATRI UTSAV - 2018

For the second year again we appointed Mr. Shushil chauhan of Kala Smruti Group for Conducting Navratri Utsav (Garba and Dandiya raas) 10th October 2018 to 18th October 2018, (for 9 days) at our ground, plot no. 198A. It was free for all residents of Goregaon area. This navratri program was very much appreciated by all section of people and they liked our navratri program. Indian Playback Singer Shri Sudesh Bhosle also visited our Navratri Utsav.

Ganpati UTSAV - 2023

Ganesh Chaturthi is a Hindu festival which holds utmost importance in the religion. This festival is celebrated following the Hindu Mythology which says that Ganesh Chaturthi is the birthday of the Lord Ganesha. Hindus refer to Lord Ganesha as the remover of all obstacles.
