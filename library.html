“THE LIBRARY- BRAIN’S BEST FRIEND”

In the Library you often find people close their mouth and open their mind’s. Libraries are essential in a process of giving citizen access to knowledge. In digital times they are needed more than ever before.

More importantly, however, libraries are the place where you can expect smart and clear answers to even most difficult questions. Neil Gaiman an English author perfectly describes what’s happening in digital times: “Google can bring you back 100,000 answers, a librarian can bring you back the right one.”
