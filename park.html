Jogger's Park

PARK

“7 DAY’S WITHOUT RUNNING MAKES ONE WEAK”

One of the favorite things to do before starting the day is to take a jog in the local park of The Jawahar Nagar Co-Operative Housing Society Limited situated at plot no 198A next to I.B.Patel school.

Jogging in the park during early hours opens up opportunities to see different scenes & portraits and open the mind to a new perspective. The park offers a variety of scenery, which makes the jog more worthwhile.
